Riri & Coco

31 January 2020 - 2 February 2020

Riri and Coco is a game created in 48 hours during the Global Game Jam Var. The theme was "Duality", and we were the same team as in our previous Game Jam with the game "Grandpa's Secrets". Our team consists of six multidisciplinary members. Riri and Coco is a two-player game that combines puzzle-solving and quick thinking, where cooperation is essential to pass as many rooms as possible before time runs out. We aimed to create a game for a wide audience, focusing on gameplay and collaboration. Riri is a circle while Coco is a square, contrasting in design and action capabilities. We integrated 11 different rooms that appear in a random order. Following the jury's decision, we won the Murex award (a festival for digital and creative cultures in Toulon), which allows us to have a booth to present our game in July 2022. We decided to continue developing the game to ensure stability during demonstrations and to address any design issues. We tested our game extensively to gather feedback for improvement before presenting it at the festival. You can find the first version of Riri and Coco on the Global Game Jam website. Feel free to provide your feedback!
