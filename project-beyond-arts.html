Beyond arts

2020-Still in progress

Beyond arts is a project realized during the second year of the engineering school ENJIMIN-CNAM. The concept was to be able to propose concerts in virtual reality in an accessible way to the most artists possible. We wanted to break the barrier between the artist and the audience by offering various environments and interactions depending on the artist's performance. Other than a simple concert, we wanted to offer a real experience to the audience. They could make their own mix, because all instruments were spatialized in the environment. Therefore, we realized a first demonstration in November, which integrates different functionalities:

  • A virtual environment
  • The connection interfaces to the different concerts
  • Concert creation interfaces with options for collaboration between artists (played with an artist who has recorded music with an instrument)
  • The multiplayer in virtual reality with vocal interaction (photon)
  • A java API and a database for registration and concert creation
  • HRTF spatialized audio with Wwise and Google Resonances
  • Interactions with the artist's Twitch chat
