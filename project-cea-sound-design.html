Automating of Sound Design in VR workflow

September 2019 - September 2022

During my last year as an apprentice at CEA I had to realize my final project on "Automating of Sound Design in VR workflow". In our team, no one was destined to do Sound design. Our old simulations had no sound environment. After several tests, the users pointed out this defect, because it marred the immersion in the simulations. That is when I started to do sound design in the different simulations. My workflow was the following: I recorded the sounds or voices with a zoom then I edit them in Cubase to then integrate them in Wwise (with the resonnance plug-in) which is directly linked to Unity. However, the goal was to be able to automate as much as possible and to optimize this workflow. Therefore, I'm designing a system where the sounds are modified according to the speed, mass and material of the object. I will also create several atmospheres depending on the environment in which we are. This project allows me to familiarize myself with sound immersion, because for the moment I was more focused on visual immersion. For this final project, I will have to lead a team, do user testing and have an innovative approach.
