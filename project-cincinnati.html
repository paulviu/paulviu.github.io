Cincinnati

June 2021 - Still in progress

Cincinnati is a Tactical RPG where the player controls a team of burglars who seek revenge on a mysterious character after a robbery gone wrong. The player will follow this team throughout their burglary adventures. In order to carry out their misdeeds, the team will have to go through several phases: the preparation, the heist and the exfiltration. To carry out the heist, the team members use their own skills and benefit from the help of mercenaries in order to carry out more and more spectacular heists.

This game is made on our free time using Unreal engine 5 technology. I am the project manager and developer of the team consisted of 8 people. It is composed of two 3D Artists, a 2D Artist, a Sound Designer, a Game Designer and 3 Developers. The conception of a game design document is finished and the development has started. The goal is to have a vertical slice for December.
