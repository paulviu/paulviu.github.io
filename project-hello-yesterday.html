Hello Yesterday

February 2020 - June 2020

"Hello yesterday" is a 2-player VR escape game made for a school project. The story takes place in a world where all art is forbidden, but you are part of the resistance. You will have to travel through several eras to recover lost artifacts that will overthrow the system in place. The actions you take in one era may have actions in another. You will have to play with the temporality to solve all the puzzles. To make the environment immersive, we had to gather as much information as possible about the different eras. To do this, we drew up a survey to find out what details marked their generation. I was in charge of the development in virtual reality with steam vr. I also designed the puzzles and the level design of the different eras. My team was composed of a 3D artist and a sound designer who was in charge of the artistic part. On this project I improved in development, but also in design with the writing of the different persona and the survey that guided our design choices.
