Multiplayer Workplace Organization Training

3 April 2019 - 23 June 2021

I did my internship of the 2nd year of IUT in Computer Science for twelve weeks in the training pole of the Union of Industries and Trades of the Metallurgy (UIMM) of Istres in the development of virtual reality training for alternating students. During this internship, I developed two training courses.

The first one had as objective the priming of a dosing pump. I had as input a paper range of the operation that I had to adapt for virtual reality. This training was divided in 2 parts. In the first place, the user had to realize the operation with the written and sound instructions. In a second time questions are asked to the user to verify that he is well understood the operation. The trainers can then output different statistics such as time or common errors. To certify the effectiveness of the application 33 people tested it. This allowed me to improve the application based on their feedback.

The second training is a collaborative serious game with three people. The objective of this training is to present the 5s method for the continuous improvement of tasks performed in companies. The users will have to order, tidy, discover anomalies, highlight tools in different environments (workshop, storeroom, office). Each player will be in an environment where he will have to perform certain tasks. Then they all meet in a room where they discuss and vote if the different tasks are valid.
This was my first professional experience in computer science and virtual reality. I realized all development alone, which taught me to be autonomous. While respecting the specifications that I had written with my internship master.
